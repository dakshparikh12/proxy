Same §3.3 paragraph asserts two things about voice: (a) "*A direct answer can fire a ≤500ms canned 'on it' spoken line **the instant it's picked up***" (encoded as `AC-SPEAK-09` p95≤500 ms from pickup, `AC-SPEAK-10` canned), and (b) "*speaking only begins on a boundary signal (3.6)*" (encoded as `AC-SPEAK-06` / `AC-TURN-05`, P0, `speak_without_boundary_allowed: 0`).
